Comic

Several comic works I created since 2017. The majority of them are black and white, but I have also sometimes experimenting with colored comics.

Worldbuilding

My main hobby is actually doing fantasy worldbuilding. My main worldbuilding project is Tagalbuni, but there are also several other projects I am currently developing.

The project consists of many aspects of worldbuilding: cartography, magic and technology systems, culture and language, and also some speculative biology.
